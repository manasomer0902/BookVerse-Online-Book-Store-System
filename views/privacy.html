Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at BookVerse.

1. Information We Collect

When you use BookVerse, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used only to:

  • Process your book orders
  • Send order confirmation and updates via email
  • Deliver hard copy books or soft copy (PDF) books

3. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information. Passwords are stored in encrypted form and payment details are not stored on our servers.

4. Information Sharing

BookVerse does not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used strictly for order-related purposes.

5. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials. Please ensure that your contact details are accurate while placing orders.

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
